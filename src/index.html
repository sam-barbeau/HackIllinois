Sleep Tracker


Sleep is very important, yet many people do not get enough of it. In fact, 50% of college students report daytime sleepiness, and 70% report insufficient sleep. The GPAS of students receiving 9+ hours of sleep per night were significantly higher (3.24) than those of students receiving 6 or fewer hours of sleep per night (2.74). Use this tracker to log your sleep throughout the week!

Protein Tracker


Many athletes, bodybuilders, and those focusing on good nutritional health track protein consumption. According to Mayo Clinic The recommended amount of daily protein intake to prevent deficiency for an average adult is 0.8 grams per kilogram of body weight. Use this tracker to help you track your protein intake throughout the day. Note, if you exercise or if you are above the ages of 40-50 your daily protein needs increase in order to support muscle growth and prevent losing muscle mass.
